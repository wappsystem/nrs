Welcome to the screening website for the research study "non-restorative sleep".
You are invited to take part in this research study. This research aims to learn more about insomnia, a disorder of short and/or non-restorative sleep.

Eligibility
We seek specific volunteers that meet our selection criteria. You may be eligible to participate in this study if you:
• have completed the screening questionnaires on this website
• are aged between 18-65 years
• have normal sleep and waking hours
• are able to be in a MRI scanner i.e. no internal metal fragments, claustrophobia or (potential) pregnancy
• do not have a sleep disorder other than insomnia (e.g. sleep apnea)

Your participation in this study is voluntary.

Eligible participants will be contacted and invited to continue the screening process.

If you like to know more about this research study before completing these screening questionnaires, you can contact the study-coordinator at rick.wassing@sydney.edu.au.
